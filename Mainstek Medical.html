Mainstek Medical

Mainstek Medical CC is a Namibian-registered healthcare and medical supply company based in Windhoek.
The company specializes in providing high-quality medical consumables, reliable procurement services, professional medical practitioner support,
and expert installation, maintenance, and repair of laboratory and medical equipment.
Mainstek serves hospitals, clinics, diagnostic laboratories, and other healthcare institutions, offering tailored solutions to meet their operational and clinical needs.
